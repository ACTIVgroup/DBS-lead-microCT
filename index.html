Micro-CT images of deep brain stimulation leads

Acquired with a Bruker Skyscan 1276 system by the ACTIV group at the University Medical Center Freiburg.
The units of the scale bar represent the number of voxels. The voxel size is between 20 and 35 µm depending on the lead (scanner magnification was adjusted for each sample to optimize image quality).
To interact with an image, click on the menu icon in the top left corner.
Images can be downloaded by clicking on the download icon.
Images are downscaled. They can be downloaded in full resolution (between 4 and 7 µm voxel size) from the Zenodo repository (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.10245469).
