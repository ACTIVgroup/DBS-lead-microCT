Micro-CT images of deep brain stimulation leads

Acquired with a Bruker Skyscan 1276 system.
The units of the scale bar represent the number of voxels. The voxel size is 25 x 25 x 25 µm.
To interact with an image, click on the menu icon on the top left corner.
Images can be downloaded by clicking on the download icon.
Images on this website are downscaled. They can be downloaded in full resolution (5 µm voxel size) from the Zenodo repository (DOI ...).
